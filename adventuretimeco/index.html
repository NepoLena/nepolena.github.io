Adventure Time Co.

Day 0

  • arrive at Almaty airport
  • check in at hotel
  • (no dinner provided since the plane arrives after closing of the hotel kitchens)

Day 1

  • breakfast: hotel, smorgasbord
  • trip to Altyn Emel National park
  • check in at first accommodation, Kalinino village (Басши)
  • visit the Singing dunes
  • lunch: pre-packed picnic box
  • free time, outdoor picnic till late afternoon
  • dinner: at the guesthouse

Day 2

  • breakfast: served by the guest house
  • trip to Aktau and Katutau mountain range
  • lunch: at the guest house
  • after lunch - departure to Chundzha
  • swim/bath in Chundzha Hot Springs, natural thermal water
  • check in to next guest house (in Chundzha)
  • dinner: at the guest house

Day 3

  • breakfast: at the guest house
  • departure to Narynkol town (Нарынқол)
  • set up camp
  • lunch: at nearby restaurant (serving traditional Kazakh food)
  • trekking along the Bayankol river (Баянкол)
  • dinner: food at late night campfire

Day 4

  • breakfast: at the camp
  • trekking around lake Kara Kul (Қара көл),
    from here you can see the Khan Tengri peak
  • lunch: when needed while trekking, pre-prepared lunchboxes
  • dinner: at camp

Day 5

  • breakfast: at camp
  • moving to Saty village(Саты), check into the guest house
  • lunch: at a local restaurant
  • looking at Kaindy lake (Каинды)
  • dinner: provided by the guest house

Day 6

  • breakfast: at the guesthouse
  • transfer to Kolsai lake (Көлсай көлдері)
  • hiking to the lake
  • lunch: outdoor picnic
  • horse riding around the lake
  • hike back
  • dinner: at the guesthouse

Day 7

  • breakfast: early breakfast at the guesthouse
  • travel to Charyn Canyon
  • departure to Almaty
  • lunch: late lunch at a restaurant of choice in Almaty
  • departure from the airport
